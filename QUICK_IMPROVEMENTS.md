## 📞 Decision Framework

**Ask yourself:**
1. **Who are my users?** → Determines priority
2. **What's my timeline?** → Determines scope
3. **What's my budget?** → Determines resources
4. **What's my goal?** → Determines features

**Example:**
- Users: Law students → Focus on UX, export
- Timeline: 1 month → Do quick wins first
- Budget: Solo dev → Prioritize high ROI
- Goal: Portfolio project → Focus on unique features
